About Me

My Name is Mark Suverkrup, and I am a Software Engineer. After exploring the depths of the Financial Services World, I have turned to coding as it is both my passion and my desired career. I am a fast-thinking, problem-solving machine that is able to work independently or collaboratively to accomplish complex goals. I love learning, and go out my way to decipher the ins and outs of whatever I am asked to do. I hope to take these skills to a company focused on disruptive innovation and industry changing technology.

I am a graduate of Hanover College with a double major in Economics and Business, as well as a graduate of the Eleven Fifty Academy. I am also licenced in the securities field holding designations Series SIE, 6, 63, and 65.
